MOANA

MOANA is the daughter of Chief Tui of Motunui She is a strong and caring sixteen-year-old who wants to explore the ocean a gainst her father's whishes.

HEIHEI is the slow-witted village rooster that accidentally stow away on Moana's seafaring quest. Maui nicknames him Drumstick.
